## Introduction

The Wallace Collection is a museum located in London, England, which houses an extensive collection of fine and decorative arts. The collection was amassed by Sir Richard Wallace in the 19th century and bequeathed to the nation upon his death. It includes paintings, sculptures, furniture, arms and armor, and other works of art from across Europe and Asia.

## Key Pieces of Artwork

### Trophy Head (Asante)

![https://www.wallacecollection.org/media/images/OA1683_1_HcFAF7g.width-2000.jpg](https://www.wallacecollection.org/media/images/OA1683_1_HcFAF7g.width-2000.jpg)

- Author: Unknown
- Date: Purchased by Sir Richard Wallace in c1874
- Description: Originating from Ghana, this artifact represents a significant piece of African art and history.

### Self-Portrait in a Black Cap

[https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcQ0GtfHcNtunUYuSSd34X2tBouyjp7eOwQLmD0umX4Js5-gLEzyZFZHctSOZ6VbVtVAbs4&usqp=CAU](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcQ0GtfHcNtunUYuSSd34X2tBouyjp7eOwQLmD0umX4Js5-gLEzyZFZHctSOZ6VbVtVAbs4&usqp=CAU)

- Author: Rembrandt
- Date: 1637
- Description: One of over sixty self-portraits by Rembrandt, this painting features the artist in a 16th-century fur cape and leather glove, symbolizing a gentleman.

### The Swing

![https://i.ytimg.com/vi/3FjjP7Uyb1U/maxresdefault.jpg](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/3FjjP7Uyb1U/maxresdefault.jpg)

- Author: Jean-Honore Fragonard
- Date: c 1765
- Description: A mildly erotic painting, it depicts a girl swinging and kicking off her shoe in front of her old husband and young lover.

### Nelly O'Brien

![https://wallacelive.wallacecollection.org/eMP/eMuseumPlus?service=DynamicAsset&sp=SU5mxm4Yx%2FVbhp94nksEmWhUPPCPARCxmRF3wZoiekBYI9dLioBnZzXySIRTomizoxxT9oo9OlonT%0APnyO6EhNhnOgOpQYyGgGmp9f4Yzsn%2FRogeai2vDoqTtzaKEj2%2BxNxIqE9mglfu4%3D&sp=Simage%2Fjpeg](https://wallacelive.wallacecollection.org/eMP/eMuseumPlus?service=DynamicAsset&sp=SU5mxm4Yx%2FVbhp94nksEmWhUPPCPARCxmRF3wZoiekBYI9dLioBnZzXySIRTomizoxxT9oo9OlonT%0APnyO6EhNhnOgOpQYyGgGmp9f4Yzsn%2FRogeai2vDoqTtzaKEj2%2BxNxIqE9mglfu4%3D&sp=Simage%2Fjpeg)

- Date: c 1762
- Description: A portrait of an actress and High Courtesan, known for her direct gaze, which suggests frankness, forthrightness, and power.

### Perdita - Mrs. Robinson

![https://wallacelive.wallacecollection.org/eMP/eMuseumPlus?service=DynamicAsset&sp=SU5mxm4Yx%2FVbhp94nksEmWhUPPCPARCxmRF3wZoiekBYI9dLioBnZzXySIRTomizoxxT9oo9OlonT%0APnyO6EhNhnOgOpQYyGgGmp9f4Yzsn%2FRogeai2vDoqdRiWIHYKMuWxIqE9mglfu4%3D&sp=Simage%2Fjpeg](https://wallacelive.wallacecollection.org/eMP/eMuseumPlus?service=DynamicAsset&sp=SU5mxm4Yx%2FVbhp94nksEmWhUPPCPARCxmRF3wZoiekBYI9dLioBnZzXySIRTomizoxxT9oo9OlonT%0APnyO6EhNhnOgOpQYyGgGmp9f4Yzsn%2FRogeai2vDoqdRiWIHYKMuWxIqE9mglfu4%3D&sp=Simage%2Fjpeg)

- Author: Sir Joshua Reynolds
- Date: 1780s
- Description: A portrait of an actress from 'Winter's Tale' and mistress of the Prince of Wales.

### Dance to the Music of Time

![https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f1/The_dance_to_the_music_of_time_c._1640.jpg/1200px-The_dance_to_the_music_of_time_c._1640.jpg](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f1/The_dance_to_the_music_of_time_c._1640.jpg/1200px-The_dance_to_the_music_of_time_c._1640.jpg)

- Author: Nicolas Poussin
- Date: 1634-6
- Description: An allegorical painting that represents the four seasons or four conditions - poverty, labor, riches, pleasure, and back to poverty - in a cycle of time.

### Laughing Cavalier

![https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/97/Cavalier_soldier_Hals-1624x.jpg/1200px-Cavalier_soldier_Hals-1624x.jpg](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/97/Cavalier_soldier_Hals-1624x.jpg/1200px-Cavalier_soldier_Hals-1624x.jpg)

- Author: Frans Hals
- Date: 1624
- Description: A study in blacks, this portrait features a jovial and well-dressed gentleman.

### Views of Venice

![https://www.wallacecollection.org/media/images/P499_c53feSQ.width-2000.jpg](https://www.wallacecollection.org/media/images/P499_c53feSQ.width-2000.jpg)

- Author: Antonio Canaletto
- Date: Mid-18th century
- Description: Numerous examples of Canaletto's depictions of Venice can be found in the Main Gallery and side room.

### Rainbow Landscape

![https://www.wallacecollection.org/media/images/P63_XJjAW4g.width-2000.jpg](https://www.wallacecollection.org/media/images/P63_XJjAW4g.width-2000.jpg)

- Author: Peter Paul Rubens
- Date: 1636
- Description: A lush and vibrant landscape painting by the renowned Flemish artist.

### Armour as Art

![https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/2298/2943/products/TWC-11_1200x630.jpg?v=1547122512](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/2298/2943/products/TWC-11_1200x630.jpg?v=1547122512)

- Author: Unknown
- Date: c 1480
